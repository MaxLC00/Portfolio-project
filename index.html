About Me

My name is Maxwell Laskey-Cthaeh but I prefer going by Max LC, because my name is so hard to spell (even if you were Hooked on Phonics). I am a multimedia artist, having studies writing and performance at Columbia College Chicago and worked throughout the Chicagoland area as a writer, performer, and stage manager. I also worked as a Graphic Designer and Pediatric Emergency Medical Technician in my (not so) free time. I decided to learn to code (C#, CSS, HTML, Javascript) so that i could make interactive art pieces, web applications, and games because the format allows for complex projects to reach large audiences without a reduction in quality.
